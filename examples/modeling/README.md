# Modeling Examples

The modeling prompts in this kit were integrated from the local
`openclaw-modeling-factory` workspace.

That source workspace contains generated KSML examples for domains such as:

- pet hospital management
- dental clinic management
- warehouse inventory management
- equipment maintenance management
- training school management
- restaurant chain management
- hotel operations management
- vehicle rental management
- property management
- fitness center management
- moving company management
- cloud computing services

This kit keeps the entry repository lightweight. Use the examples in the source
workspace as reference material when available, but treat
`prompts/modeling/ksml-rules.md` as the rules source of truth.
